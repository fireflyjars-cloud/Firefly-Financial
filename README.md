## 📖 The Jarring Framework

The framework is inspired by the **Torah** — from the Hebrew root word *Yarah*, meaning to shoot an arrow straight and hit the mark. Torah means God’s teaching and instruction. The Jarring Framework applies that same principle to your finances: a straight path, clearly marked.

Your income flows first into **The Lamp** — the Living Account — and from there transfers into five jars, each named after a book of the Torah.

### 🪔 The Lamp™️ (Living Account)

The Lamp™️ is not a jar. It is the user’s **main account** — where income is received and fixed living expenses are paid from. The goal is to keep The Lamp™️ at **50% or less of your net income**, freeing the remaining 50% to flow into the five jars. Reaching that threshold is the transformation milestone — the moment breathing room and wealth-building begin.

-----

## 🎯 The Under-50% Goal — The Transformation Milestone

Most people have no idea how much of their income is being consumed by living expenses. Rent, utilities, subscriptions, car payments — it quietly adds up to 70%, 80%, oftentimes more. And they wonder why there’s nothing left at the end of the month.

The Firefly system draws a clear line: **your fixed living expenses must be 50% or less of your net income.**

That single shift changes everything.

When your Living Account (The Lamp) drops below 50%, the other 50% flows freely into the five jars — investing, playing, learning, saving, and giving. That’s when the system stops feeling like a budget and starts feeling like a wealth-building engine.

Getting there isn’t instant. For most people it requires intentional decisions — downsizing, eliminating expenses, increasing income, or all three. But the Firefly system walks you through it step by step, and the community walks with you.

**This is not just a budgeting tip. It is the threshold between surviving and building.**

## 🌟 Phase 1 — What We’re Building

Phase 1 is a **web app** (browser-based, built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript). No bank connections. Manual entry only. Fast to build, easy to use.

- **ASMR Pour Animation** — liquid flows visually from The Lamp (Living Account) into all five jars when you log income
- **12-Level Journey** — progress through 12 levels as you work the system: First Flicker → Flicker → Ember → Slow Burn → Flame → Torch → Lantern → Beacon → Wildfire → Swarm → Luminance → Firefly

- **Weekly Quests** — small challenges that build the habit
- **Milestone Rewards** — celebrate every breakthrough
- **Community Feed** — accountability with people on the same path
- **Content Hub** — teachings, guides, and tools rooted in the Firefly framework
