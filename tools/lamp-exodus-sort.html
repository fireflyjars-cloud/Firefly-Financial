The Lamp & Exodus Sort™

Needs · Wants · The 50% Clarity Exercise

The Jarring Framework starts with one question: what is actually feeding The Lamp? Your Lamp is your main account — the home of every fixed living expense. The goal is to keep it at 50% or less of your net income so the other half can flow freely into the five jars. Use this tool to sort what you spend into two honest lists — what is a true Lamp expense (a need that keeps life running) and what belongs in your Exodus Jar (joy, play, and desire). Then see your number.
