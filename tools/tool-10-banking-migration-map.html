The Self-Employment APY Unlock

Since you and your husband are paid daily from self-employment, you don't have a payroll direct deposit. To earn the 3.80% APY, you need to meet one of these: transfer $5,000+ into SoFi every 31-day period, OR subscribe to SoFi Plus. Step-by-Step Migration Guide

The exact order of operations — do these in sequence, not all at once.

⏱️

Timeline: 45–60 Days Total

This is not a weekend project. Rushing a banking migration means broken autopays and missed payments. Follow the phases. Phase 1 and 2 happen in parallel. Phase 3 starts only after one full billing cycle on SoFi.

1

PHASE 1 — Separate Business from Personal

Before touching SoFi, get your business money completely separate from your personal money. This is the foundation everything else sits on.

  • Confirm your existing business checking is in the name of Clear Cut Window Tinting or your LLC — not your personal name
  • If it's commingled, open a free business checking account (Relay Bank is free, Colorado-based businesses use it widely)
  • Verify ALL Square deposits are routing to the business account only
  • Designate a "pay yourself" day (every Friday is common for self-employed couples)
  • Decide your weekly paycheck amount — this is what flows into The Lamp™ each week
⚠️ Do not move on to Step 2 until Square is confirmed routing to the business account.
2

Open Your SoFi Checking + Savings Account

  • Go to sofi.com/banking — opens in about 10 minutes
  • Open a single SoFi Checking + Savings account — this is both accounts in one
  • Fund it with a small initial transfer ($100–$500) from your current CU to activate it
  • You'll receive your SoFi debit card in 7–10 business days
  • Your husband should open his own SoFi account separately (or you can add him as a joint account holder)
📝 SoFi requires no minimum balance to open and charges no monthly fees.
3

Create Your 5 Vaults Inside SoFi

In the SoFi app: Savings → Vaults → Create New Vault. Name each one exactly:

  • 🌱 Genesis Jar™ — Investing & Multiplication
  • 🚪 Exodus Jar™ — Playing & Joy
  • 📜 Leviticus Jar™ — Learning & Self-Development
  • 🔢 Numbers Jar™ — Savings & Discipline
  • 📣 Deuteronomy Jar™ — Giving & Generosity
All 5 vaults earn the same APY as your main savings balance. No extra accounts, no extra rates to track.
4

Move Your Monthly Bills to SoFi (One at a Time)

Make a list of every bill on autopay. Move them to SoFi one by one over 2–3 weeks:

  • Log into each biller's website and update the payment method to your SoFi checking account/routing number
  • OR call the biller and provide SoFi routing + account number
  • Note: SoFi routing number is 031101279
  • Keep a spreadsheet: Bill name → Old account → Moved to SoFi → Date confirmed
  • Do NOT close CU accounts until every autopay has successfully processed through SoFi at least once
🗂️ Common bills to migrate: utilities, subscriptions, insurance, phone, internet, streaming services, gym, any recurring payment.
5

Run Your First Weekly Pour

Once SoFi is open and vaults are created — do your first manual jar pour:

  • Transfer your weekly "paycheck" from business checking into SoFi Checking
  • Use the Weekly Calculator (Tab 2) to calculate each vault amount
  • In SoFi app: tap each vault and transfer the calculated amount from your main savings balance
  • Watch the vaults fill — this is the system working
This is Pour Day. Every week, same day. It takes 5 minutes when the system is running.
6

Verify the $5,000/Month Qualifying Threshold

To earn 3.80% APY without a traditional direct deposit:

  • SoFi requires $5,000+ in qualifying deposits every 31 days
  • Your weekly paycheck transfers from your business account likely count — confirm with SoFi support when you open (ask: "Do incoming ACH transfers from my own business account count as qualifying deposits?")
  • If your combined income in any given month is under $5,000, the rate drops — plan accordingly
  • SoFi Plus subscription ($10/month) also unlocks the full APY regardless of deposit amount — worth it if your income varies significantly
7

PHASE 3 — Wind Down Credit Union Accounts

Only do this after one full billing cycle (30–45 days) on SoFi with zero payment failures:

  • Confirm all autopays have processed successfully through SoFi
  • Transfer remaining balances from CU accounts to SoFi
  • Request account closure in writing or in person at each Credit Union
  • Keep one CU account open for 30 additional days as a fallback (zero balance is fine)
  • Cancel any CU debit cards after the final account is closed
🎉 When the last CU account closes, the migration is complete. Your money is now lit.

SoFi Vault Setup Guide

Exact names, purposes, and rules for each vault — and how to use SoFi's 20-vault capacity for sub-goals.

🔧 HOW TO CREATE A VAULT IN SOFI

  1. Open the SoFi app on your phone
  2. Tap Banking at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll to your Savings section → tap Vaults
  4. Tap + Create a Vault
  5. Enter the vault name exactly as listed below
  6. Optionally set a savings goal amount
  7. Tap Save
  8. Repeat for all 5 primary jars
Vault Name Allocation Rule & Purpose
🌱 Genesis Jar™Investing & Multiplication 10% Locked until your investment goal is reached. This jar feeds your future — stock account, real estate fund, or business investment. Do not touch it for anything else.
🚪 Exodus Jar™Playing & Joy 10% Spent completely every month — no carrying over. This defeats the "I deserve it" impulse spending by giving joy a proper container. Dining out, date night, fun purchases — all come from here.
📜 Leviticus Jar™Learning & Self-Development 10% Books, courses, certifications, seminars. You are your greatest asset. This jar invests in the person running the business — both of you.
🔢 Numbers Jar™Savings & Discipline 10% Builds your financial cushion and funds named future purchases. Create sub-vaults for specific goals: Emergency Fund, New Equipment, Vehicle, Vacation. Never touch it for impulse purchases.
📣 Deuteronomy Jar™Giving & Generosity 10% Tithes, offerings, charity, blessing others. Giving blesses the whole system — this jar is not optional. It is the jar that keeps the system moving.

As self-employed business owners, you owe quarterly estimated taxes. Create a Tax Reserve vault inside Numbers and transfer approximately 25–30% of your net business profit each week into it. This is money that was never yours to spend. Having it in a named vault — earning interest — eliminates tax-time panic completely.
