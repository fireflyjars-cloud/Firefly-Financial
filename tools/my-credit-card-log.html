See Every Purchase, Stay Out of the Trap

A credit card is a tool, not an income stream. Every charge is a promise to pay — with your future income, your Lamp budget, your jar allocations. This log makes every purchase visible before the bill arrives, so nothing surprises you, nothing carries over, and the card stays a tool instead of becoming a trap.

🪔 The Firefly Credit Card Rule

🪔

In the Firefly Financial system, a credit card used correctly is paid in full every month. That means every charge logged here must have a payment date — and that payment date must be before or on the due date. If you cannot write a payment date on a purchase, ask whether it belongs on the card or whether it belongs in a jar. Carried balances inflate The Lamp and dim the light.
