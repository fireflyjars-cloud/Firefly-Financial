✦ What to Watch For in Any Offer

📊

APR Is Everything

The interest rate determines how much you really pay. A lower APR on a longer loan can cost more than a higher APR on a shorter one.

⚠️

Prepayment Penalties

Some lenders charge you for paying off early. In the Firefly system, you want to pay off debt fast — avoid any loan that punishes you for it.

🎯

Introductory Rates Expire

A 0% intro APR on a credit card becomes a high APR the day it ends. Know the real rate and plan for it before signing.

🪙

Monthly Payment vs. The Lamp

Every new payment raises your Lamp %. Before borrowing, ask: does this monthly payment keep me at or below the 50% Transformation Milestone?
